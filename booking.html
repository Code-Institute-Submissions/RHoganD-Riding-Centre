Book your ride

Please choose your activity and then your riding ability (please be honest!) and finally the date and time you wish to come. Do not overstate your riding ability as it is both dangerous for you and others. You will not be allowed to ride if you are not the correct standard for your chosen activity and no refunds will be given

Our instructors are BHS qualified and Our aim is to help you enjoy and understand horses. All our horses are used for dressage, showjumping and cross-country, working at a variety of levels to suit the rider and have very good temperament.

  • Indoor and outdoor arena
  • 63 years of experience
  • Training for the British Horse Society (BHS) Examinations.
